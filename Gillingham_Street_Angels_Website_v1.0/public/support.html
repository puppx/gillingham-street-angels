Every donation makes a difference in our community

Donating to Gillingham Street Angels

Our support is a lifeline for thousands of people in our community and surrounding areas, their families and pets. In these unprecedented times the Gillingham Street Angels are working hard to support the most vulnerable in our society, from our homeless, our young through to our older generation including pets, those who suffer from social isolation, or those who are simply overwhelmed by the challenges associated with the Covid-19 pandemic. We can only continue to provide these services with your help. Whether it's a one-off donation or a regular gift, no donation is too small to make a difference, so please donate today.

Help us be there for more animals

We do everything we can to alleviate animal suffering or end up in rescue centres because families find that they can no longer afford to keep their beloved pet due to food costs or health care, we cannot support this alone. It's the generosity of supporters like you who make it possible. You need to know that we strongly appreciate your support to protect and care for animals in need.

How your donations help

We provide support with food (for pets as well) and household goods for members of the Medway community, and surrounding areas. By making a donation, you're helping us to help those in need within our community and further afield. We hope to reduce waste as much as possible and see everything distributed and re-used, to help people who may not have essential items like beds, storage for clothes, or a table to eat dinner from. There are a few decorative bits too, to cheer up your space as mental health means such a lot to us.
