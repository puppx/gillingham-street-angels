Volunteering with Gillingham Street Angels

Our volunteers are at the heart of everything we do

Joining the Gillingham Street Angels team as a volunteer is a rewarding way to give back to your community. Our volunteers help us deliver vital services to those in need, from distributing food parcels to helping in our charity shops and warehouses.

All of our roles are fast paced and do require commitment, they are not easy roles but they are very rewarding ones. We provide training and support to all our volunteers to ensure they feel confident and valued in their role.

Volunteer Opportunities

Warehouse & Sorting Volunteers

Are you feeling strong, like to be active, we are looking for volunteers to also join our team at our storage unit, not only to sort through donations dropped in or collected but to keep items organised in the warehouse.

Cafe Volunteers

We are currently looking for volunteers to cook, serve and help with general kitchen duties, experience not essential but would be an advantage in working in a fast paced environment as we can get quite busy, we are mainly looking for people to cover a few hours on Saturday and Sunday at present, however other shifts on weekdays do become available.

Foodbank Distribution Volunteers

We are always looking for volunteers at our Gillingham Hub to help with our food parcel distribution. Experience not essential but would be preferred.
